The Origin

The Hills of
Gorkha, Nepal

Our farm sits on 30 hectares of fertile hill land at 750 – 1,100 m altitude, fed by natural springs and nurtured by local hands. The terrain above — built from an actual elevation map — shows every ridge and valley where your moringa grows.

Around 8,000 moringa trees grow alongside black turmeric, sweet orange, mango, lychee, and banana. High-altitude cultivation in clean air gives our crops richer phytochemical profiles than any lowland farm.
