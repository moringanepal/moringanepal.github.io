Frequently Asked Questions

What is Moringa Nepal?

Moringa Nepal is a company based in Nepal that specializes in producing and selling high-quality moringa products, including moringa powder, moringa tea, and moringa capsules.

Where is Moringa Nepal located?

We are located in Nepal, a country known for its rich biodiversity and ideal conditions for growing nutrient-dense moringa plants.

What products do you offer?

We offer three main products: moringa powder, moringa tea, and moringa capsules, all made from 100% natural and sustainably harvested moringa leaves.

What are the health benefits of moringa?

Moringa is rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. It supports immune function, promotes healthy skin, improves digestion, and helps boost energy levels naturally.

How can I purchase your products?

You can purchase our products through our online store or visit our physical outlets in Nepal. We also offer shipping for international customers.

Are your products organic?

Yes, all of our moringa products are organically grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers or pesticides, ensuring they are as natural and healthy as possible.
