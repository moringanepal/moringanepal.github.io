About Us

Organic superfoods from the heart of Nepal

Our farm sits on 30 hectares of fertile hill land at 750–1100m altitude, fed by natural springs and nurtured by local hands.

What We Grow

~8,000 moringa trees, black turmeric, turmeric, and thousands of fruit trees—sweet orange, mango, lychee, and banana. High-altitude cultivation in clean, low-pollution conditions gives our crops richer phytochemical profiles.

What We Do

We turn fresh harvests into moringa powder, tea, capsules, and herbal products. Everything is processed on-site with natural spring water. We're building toward organic certification and export to India, the Middle East, and beyond.

Why Us

Large contiguous land, natural water, road-connected processing, and diversified crops. We’re scaling up with full packaging facilities while keeping quality and sustainability at the core.
